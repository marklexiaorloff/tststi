About Laguna Lions Football Club

Welcome to Laguna Lions, we are a newly formed football team, however, our founders come from the original origins of football.

Our C.E.O was born in the Sheffield (United Kingdom) of which the first ever football club in the world was formed, Sheffield Football club, 1886.

And of which has two other world-class teams also the second and third oldest teams in the world Sheffield Wednesday and Sheffield United, both teams have gained many years of respect in world class football.

This is one of the reasons we believe that Laguna Lions has the very best of pedigree football knowledge to share the skills and performance that has true genuine origins.

Our coach is from Belgium of which he has qualifications of world class, and was a former professional football player, in Europe and United Kingdom.

We also have gained a brilliant support team to give our players the professional touch to a truly amazing sport.

The name Lions was chosen as it incorporates strength, honesty, honour, loyalty, of which has been used by monarchs around the world for hundreds of years. It is, for this reason, we chose this magnificent animal to represent our club in respect to all that believe in this philosophy.

“Victoriam Robore” is “Victory in strength” as we believe by being as one gives strength to all to achieve their ambitious goals.
